Warren Buffett has announced he will retire as Chief Executive of Berkshire Hathaway at the end of the year. The veteran investor told his company’s annual meeting that he would hand over the reins to Vice Chairman Greg Abel. “I think the time has arrived where Greg should become the chief executive of the company at year end,” said Warren Buffett, 94.
